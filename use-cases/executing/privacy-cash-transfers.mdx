## Privacy Cash Transfer APIs

### Privacy Cash

#### SOL Operations
- **deposit()**: Deposit SOL into privacy pools (shield)
  - Parameters: `lamports` - Amount in lamports
  - Returns: Deposit transaction result
- **withdraw()**: Withdraw SOL from privacy pools to recipient address (unshield)
  - Parameters: `lamports` - Amount in lamports, `recipientAddress` - Recipient address
  - Returns: Withdraw transaction result
- **getPrivateBalance()**: Get private SOL balance in privacy pools
  - Returns: Balance object with `lamports` property

#### USDC Operations
- **depositUSDC()**: Deposit USDC into privacy pools (shield)
  - Parameters: `base_units` - Amount in base units (1e6 for USDC)
  - Returns: Deposit transaction result
- **withdrawUSDC()**: Withdraw USDC from privacy pools to recipient address (unshield)
  - Parameters: `base_units` - Amount in base units, `recipientAddress` - Recipient address
  - Returns: Withdraw transaction result
- **getPrivateBalanceUSDC()**: Get private USDC balance in privacy pools
  - Returns: Balance object with `base_units` property
