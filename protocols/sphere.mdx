Sphere is a payments infrastructure platform that enables seamless transfers between fiat and virtual currencies. Sphere provides APIs for customer onboarding, KYC verification, bank accounts, wallets, transfers, and virtual accounts for on-ramps and off-ramps.

## Overview

Sphere provides developers with comprehensive payment infrastructure:

- **Fiat to Crypto Transfers**: Move between fiat currencies and virtual currencies
- **On-Ramps**: Virtual accounts for accepting payments
- **Off-Ramps**: CCTP off-ramp for liquidating to fiat
- **Customer Onboarding**: KYC verification and identity management
- **Bank Accounts**: Manage bank account connections
- **Wallets**: Create and manage wallets
- **Webhooks**: Real-time event notifications
