DFlow is a comprehensive DeFi platform providing swap APIs and prediction market infrastructure on Solana. DFlow offers powerful tools for token swaps, order management, and prediction market data.

## Overview

DFlow provides developers with a unified platform for DeFi operations:

- **Swap APIs**: Imperative and declarative swap functionality with quote generation
- **Order Management**: Track and manage swap orders and their status
- **Prediction Markets**: Complete prediction market infrastructure with events, markets, and trading
- **Real-time Data**: WebSocket support for live prices, trades, and orderbook updates
- **Token & Venue Data**: Access to token lists and available swap venues
